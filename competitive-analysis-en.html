Taiwan and International Cryptocurrency Exchange Competitive Analysis

Current Major Players in the Taiwan Market

MAX (MaiCoin)
Advantages:
  • Taiwan's first compliant exchange, high brand recognition
  • Well-established New Taiwan Dollar direct deposit/withdrawal channels
  • Local customer service support
  • Easy-to-use user interface design
Disadvantages:
  • Limited number of supported cryptocurrencies (approximately 30)
  • Insufficient trading depth, limited liquidity
  • Lack of advanced trading features (such as futures, leverage, margin)
  • Relatively high trading fees
  • Lack of innovative products (such as NFT marketplace, Launchpad)
BitoPro
Advantages:
  • Faster trading speed, stable technical architecture
  • Relatively competitive fees
  • Simple and efficient KYC process
  • Good security record, no major security incidents
Disadvantages:
  • Smaller market share than MAX
  • User experience needs improvement
  • Lack of mobile application optimization
  • Lack of innovative services, mainly focused on basic trading functions

Analysis of Major International Exchanges

Binance
Advantages:
  • World's largest exchange, daily trading volume exceeding $30 billion USD
  • Comprehensive product ecosystem: spot, futures, leverage, lending, staking, NFT, etc.
  • Strong technical architecture and trading engine
  • Rich cryptocurrency support (500+) and trading pairs
  • Strong brand influence and user community
Disadvantages:
  • Inconvenient deposits/withdrawals for Taiwan users
  • Limited Chinese customer service support, time zone differences
  • Unable to meet Taiwan's unique local needs
Coinbase
Advantages:
  • NASDAQ-listed company, strong compliance
  • High trust from institutional investors
  • User-friendly interface design
  • Good security record
Disadvantages:
  • Higher transaction fees
  • Relatively delayed product innovation
  • Low focus on the Taiwan market
  • Poor Taiwan Dollar channel connectivity
OKX
Advantages:
  • High-performance trading engine, low latency
  • Extensive product support, including spot, contracts, leverage, DeFi
  • Offers diverse yield products, such as staking and yield farms
  • Abundant liquidity, suitable for institutional and retail trading
  • Strong API support, suitable for automated trading
Disadvantages:
  • Higher regulatory risks, restricted in some countries
  • Limited fiat deposit options in some regions
